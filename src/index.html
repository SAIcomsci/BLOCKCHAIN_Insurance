Insurance HUB

Insurance HUB is a Blockchain-based intermediate entity between insurance companies and the users. Insurance companies can register themselves and can share their various policies of different domains in this platform. Users, on the other hand can also register themselves and choose any policies from any companies and can insured with the selected policy.
